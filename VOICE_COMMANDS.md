# CLIMA Voice Commands — Quick Reference

> Full 170+ command reference available in [`COMMANDS.md`](./COMMANDS.md)

---

## 🎙️ How to Use

1. Click the **microphone button** (green glow, left side)
2. Speak naturally
3. CLIMA responds aloud

---

## Quick Commands by Task

### "What's the soil moisture?"
Sensor readings for any tracked metric.

### "Turn on pump"
Pump on, off, status, or timed runs like "run pump for 5 minutes".

### "Auto mode"
Switch between automatic, manual, and scheduled control modes.

### "What's the weather?"
Current conditions, 5-day forecast, rain check, and farming weather advice.

### "Plant tips"
Growing advice, watering guidance, pest control, seasonal planting, soil health tips.

### "Show alerts"
Read, count, dismiss, or check alert settings.

### "Go to analytics"
Navigate dashboard, analytics, history, alerts page, or settings.

### "System status"
Health check, AI recommendation, time, date, network status.

### "Set moisture threshold to 30"
Adjust any alert threshold or toggle settings like dark mode, sound, notifications.

### "Help"
General help or category-specific: "help with pump", "help with sensors".

---

## Tips for Best Results

- Speak clearly at a normal pace
- Use natural language — CLIMA understands full sentences
- On mobile, hold the mic close
- Reduce background noise
- Check browser microphone permissions if the mic doesn't respond

---

## AI Mode

When AI mode is enabled (Gemini or OpenRouter configured), CLIMA understands natural language and can handle complex requests, follow-up questions, and conversational commands.

**Set up:** Add API key in Settings → AI Voice Assistant or in `.env.local`.
